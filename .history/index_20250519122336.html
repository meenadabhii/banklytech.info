Valuation Approaches

Comprehensive Valuation Techniques

We employ multiple methodologies to determine the most accurate valuation for your business.

Discounted Cash Flow

Projects future cash flows and discounts them back to present value using an appropriate discount rate.

  • Most theoretically sound method
  • Forward-looking approach
  • Accounts for time value of money

Comparable Companies

Values company based on valuation multiples of similar publicly traded companies.

  • Market-based approach
  • Relatively simple to understand
  • Reflects current market conditions

Precedent Transactions

Analyzes prices paid for similar companies in past M&A transactions.

  • Real-world transaction data
  • Includes control premium
  • Useful for M&A valuations
